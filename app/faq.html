Services

Service Questions

How long does a standard wash take? +

Our standard wash typically takes 15-20 minutes depending on your vehicle size and current conditions. You can wait in our comfortable lounge or check out while we work.

Do you damage paint during washing? +

No. We use only premium, paint-safe products and professional-grade equipment. Our touchless technology and soft-touch systems protect your vehicle's finish.

Can I get my interior detailed? +

Absolutely! We offer comprehensive interior detailing packages including vacuuming, sanitizing, and conditioning. Check our pricing page for details.

What is ceramic coating and is it worth it? +

Ceramic coating is a protective layer that shields your paint from UV rays, dirt, and contaminants for up to 2 years. It's worth the investment for long-term protection and easier maintenance.

Do you work on all vehicle types? +

Yes! We service cars, trucks, SUVs, motorcycles, and even RVs. Our team has experience with all vehicle types and sizes.

Booking

Booking Questions

How do I book an appointment? +

You can book online through our website, call us at (555) 123-4567, or visit us in person. Online booking is available 24/7 with instant confirmation.

Can I reschedule or cancel? +

Yes! You can reschedule or cancel up to 24 hours before your appointment without any fees. Just contact us online or by phone.

Do you offer same-day appointments? +

Yes, depending on availability. Check our booking system for same-day slots or call us directly to confirm availability.

Is there a no-show fee? +

We don't charge a no-show fee for your first appointment. However, we do ask for 24-hour notice for cancellations or rescheduling.

How far in advance should I book? +

We recommend booking at least 1-2 weeks in advance for popular time slots. Walk-ins are welcome but may have longer wait times.

Membership

Membership Questions

What's included in membership? +

Each membership tier includes different benefits. Basic includes 4 washes/month, Pro includes unlimited washes with priority scheduling, and Premium adds monthly details and VIP support.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan? +

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect at your next billing cycle with prorated adjustments.

Are there long-term contracts? +

No contracts required. You're free to cancel anytime with 7 days notice. No hidden fees or penalties.

Do memberships include add-ons? +

Memberships include special discounts on add-ons and premium services. Members get 10-30% off depending on their tier.

Can I pause my membership? +

Yes! You can pause your membership for up to 3 months per year without losing your benefits. Perfect for travel or seasonal needs.
