Our Story

A Passion for Detail

Founded in 2015, INT216D CarWash emerged from a simple belief: your car deserves to be treated with the care and attention it requires. What started as a single location has grown into a trusted name in premium automotive care.

We've invested in the latest technology, trained our team extensively, and sourced only the finest products to ensure every vehicle that enters our facilities receives exceptional treatment.

Today, thousands of satisfied customers trust us with their most valued possessions. We're honored to be part of their automotive care journey.
